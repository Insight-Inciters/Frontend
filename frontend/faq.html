FAQ / Help

Storage & Privacy

›Do you store my writing?
No. Input is processed for the session and then deleted. You can trigger Delete Now any time.
›Do you train on my content?
Never. We do not use user content to train models.
›Do I need an account?
No. v1 does not require login.
›Can I run this offline?
Yes, when the backend is hosted locally.
›Do you track users?
No tracking pixels. Minimal, anonymous operational logs only when required; opt-out available.

Using the Product

›What file types can I upload?
For now we only accept TXT files.
›How long does analysis take?
Usually seconds for texts under ~5,000 words on typical hardware.
›What insights do I get in v1?
Keywords & themes, a sentiment overview with a short explanation, word count, and a preview snippet.
›Is there a comparison view?
Yes. Compare two texts side-by-side and see a combined chart.
›How accurate are the insights?
They are guides, not verdicts—use them to prompt reflection.
›Any size limits?
Very large files may be truncated or rejected for stability.

Export & Deletion

›What can I export?
PDF (one-page summary) or JSON (structured data).
›Can I control deletion?
Yes—choose Delete Now or let auto-delete run after analysis/export.
›What does the JSON include?
File name, word count, preview HTML, keywords, sentiment, optional emotions, and a deletion timestamp.

Accessibility & Support

›Is the interface accessible?
Keyboard navigation, alt text for charts, and a high-contrast option are included.
›How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email support with steps to reproduce and a small non-sensitive sample.

Technical & Reliability

›What if I close the tab?
A backend cleanup task purges temporary data shortly after interruption.
›Cloud storage?
Not in v1. Download exports locally.
›Which models do you use?
A controlled NLP pipeline focused on speed and privacy.

Pricing & Terms

›Is it free?
Yes, it's absolutely free.
›Terms of use?
Use responsibly. Do not upload unlawful content or sensitive personal data.
