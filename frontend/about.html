About Us

Ink Insights helps writers see what their words are already saying. We turn drafts into visual maps — themes, moods, and motifs — all while keeping your text private on your own device.

Our Purpose

Writers are often too close to their own text to notice patterns. We surface signals that enhance your editorial intuition without compromising privacy.

What We Do

  • Rapid analysis: upload text and get distilled insights in seconds
  • Clear visuals: keywords, themes, and tone summaries
  • Exportable results: one-click PDF or JSON
  • Comparison view: check tonal changes between drafts

Privacy by Design

  • Session-only processing — nothing stored online
  • No training on user content
  • No tracking or analytics pixels
  • Local-first workflow for complete data control

How It Works

  1. Upload or paste your text
  2. Run analysis through our NLP pipeline
  3. Review insights in your dashboard
  4. Export results or auto-delete immediately

Who We Serve

  • Writers & poets refining their voice
  • Editors seeking early diagnostics
  • Educators showcasing text analysis patterns

Principles

  • Clarity over complexity • Respect over prescription • Accessibility by default
  • Your words, your meaning — we simply reveal the patterns

Responsible Use

Text analysis reveals tendencies, not truths. Use these insights as creative companions — your voice remains the final authority.

Enjoy Writing!
