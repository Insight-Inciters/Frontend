About Us

Incite Insighters helps writers see what their words are already saying. We turn drafts into maps — themes, moods, and motifs — without sending a single word off your device.

Our Purpose

Writers are often too close to their text to notice patterns. We surface clear signals that inform your editorial judgment while respecting your privacy.

What We Do

  • Rapid analysis: upload/paste text and get distilled insights in seconds
  • Clear visuals: keywords & themes, sentiment overview, concise explanations
  • Exportable results: one-click PDF or JSON
  • Optional comparison: inspect two texts side-by-side for tonal changes

Privacy by Design

  • Session-only processing; immediate deletion or auto-delete
  • No training on user content
  • No tracking pixels; minimal operational logs with opt-out
  • Local-first option for teams preferring offline control

How It Works

  1. Upload a poem/prose excerpt or paste text
  2. Run analysis through our controlled NLP pipeline
  3. Review insights in a simple dashboard
  4. Export if needed, then delete immediately or let auto-delete run

Who We Serve

  • Writers & poets seeking a fast thematic and tonal check
  • Editors needing early diagnostics on focus and repetition
  • Educators demonstrating pattern-finding in class

Principles

  • Clarity over complexity • Respect over prescription • Accessibility by default
  • Honest scope: we reveal patterns; you decide what to do with them

Roadmap

  • v1: keywords & sentiment; PDF/JSON export; strict deletion
  • v1.1: emotions blend; comparison refinements; more accessibility options

Responsible Use

NLP highlights patterns, not absolute truths. Use insights to spark reflection. Your voice remains the final arbiter.
