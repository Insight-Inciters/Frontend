Writer Struggles

Every writer faces challenges from overusing certain words without noticing, to struggling with tone, or missing the hidden themes in their work. Often, the emotions you want to convey don’t quite come through on the page. These struggles are part of the creative process, but they can also hold back your writing’s impact.

That’s where insights step in, helping you analyze your text to uncover hidden messages and patterns.

Ink Insights

How are we helping them?

We built this tool to make the invisible visible. By analyzing your text, we highlight the words you repeat, reveal the themes you return to, and map out the emotions flowing through your writing. Our dashboard turns your work into simple charts and insights, helping you refine your style, balance your tone, and better understand the stories you’re telling.

What can your writing reveal?

Uncover the most repeated words in your text.
Spot overused terms and discover the vocabulary that shapes your writing.

See whether your tone leans positive, negative, or neutral.
A quick glance at the emotional direction of your work.

Identify recurring ideas and group them into themes.
Visualize how your words connect to form larger story patterns.

Explore emotional tones and sensory cues.
Understand the feelings your writing evokes through data-driven insights.

“Your writing voice is the deepest expression of who you are.” — Cheryl Strayed

How it works?

1. Upload

Upload your file, we accept .txt format.

2. Analysis

Our NLP engine scans your writing to uncover keywords, themes, sentiments, and emotional tones.

3. Dashboard

View interactive charts and clear insights. Explore your writing’s hidden layers in a friendly dashboard.

Privacy Guranteed

Your words are yours alone. We process your text securely on our local server, and once your analysis is complete, the data is deleted automatically. Nothing is stored, nothing is shared, your privacy is always protected.
